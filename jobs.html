Senior Gameplay Programmer

  • Job Reference Number: PGM01
  • Salary: $120,000 - $150,000 AUD
  • Reporting to: Lead Technical Director

Brief Description

We are seeking an experienced Senior Gameplay Programmer to architect and implement high-quality gameplay systems for our upcoming AAA title. You will work closely with designers and artists to bring our game world to life.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop, maintain, and optimize core gameplay mechanics (AI, physics, player controls).
  2. Collaborate with the design team to create compelling and interactive player experiences.
  3. Write clean, efficient, and well-documented C++ code.
  4. Mentor junior programmers and contribute to code reviews.

Essential & Preferable Requirements

  • 5+ years of professional experience in game development.
  • Expertise in C++ and experience with Unreal Engine 5.
  • Strong understanding of 3D math and physics principles.
  • (Preferred) Experience with multiplayer networking and console development.

Lead 3D Artist

  • Job Reference Number: ART01
  • Salary: $110,000 - $140,000 AUD
  • Reporting to: Art Director

Brief Description

We are looking for a creative and skilled Lead 3D Artist to guide our art team in creating stunning characters, environments, and assets. Your artistic vision will be crucial in defining the visual identity of our games.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Lead and mentor a team of 3D artists, providing feedback and direction.
  2. Establish and maintain the art production pipeline.
  3. Create high-quality 3D models, textures, and materials.
  4. Ensure artistic consistency across all game assets.

Essential & Preferable Requirements

  • 7+ years in the games industry with at least 2 years in a lead role.
  • Expert proficiency in Blender.
  • A strong portfolio showcasing exceptional modeling and texturing skills.
  • (Preferred) Experience with Blender.
