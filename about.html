Who We Are

PixelForge Studios is a team of builders who care about craft, clarity, and player joy. We mix retro aesthetics with modern tech to ship accessible, high-quality experiences.

We value collaboration, ownership, and steady iteration. Pixels are small; impact isn’t.

Our Values

Player First

We test early and often. We remove friction and earn delight through polish.

Craft over Hype

We keep scopes sane, code clean, and art readable. Trends don’t lead our roadmap.

Kind Candor

Direct feedback, respectful tone, written decisions. We optimize for team trust.

How We Work

Process

  • Weekly releases with small, reversible changes.
  • Design reviews for readability and accessibility.
  • Playtests inform priorities, not opinions.

Tech & Tools

  • Modern web stack for sites and tooling.
  • Sprite workflows built around Aseprite.
  • CI for builds, linting, and a11y checks.

Benefits

  • Health cover and paid time off
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Learning budget for tools and courses
  • Regular team jams
